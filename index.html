About

I design in the unglamorous, high-stakes corners of fintech — KYC, 2FA, compliance forms, payment flows. The places where a confused user doesn't just bounce; they abandon an investment or call support on a trading day.

As the designer on PPFAS's investor and distributor platforms, I work end-to-end: walking flows as a first-time user, mapping journeys before opening Figma, iterating with engineering, and shipping inside real regulatory constraints. Security isn't negotiable in my work — so clarity has to do the heavy lifting.
